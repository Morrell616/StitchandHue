Simple Crochet Granny Square Pattern (6 Rounds)

Abbreviations:

  • ch = chain
  • sl st = slip stitch
  • dc = double crochet

Pattern:

  1. Round 1:

    Start with a magic ring. Ch 3 (counts as the first dc), 2 dc into the ring, *ch 2, 3 dc into the ring*, repeat from * to * 2 more times, ch 2. Join with a sl st to the top of the beginning ch 3. (You should have 4 sets of 3 dc and 4 ch-2 spaces).

  2. Round 2:

    Sl st to the first ch-2 space. Ch 3 (counts as the first dc), 2 dc into the same ch-2 space, ch 2, 3 dc into the same ch-2 space (first corner made). *3 dc into the next ch-2 space, ch 2, 3 dc into the same ch-2 space*, repeat from * to * 2 more times. Join with a sl st to the top of the beginning ch 3. (You should have 8 sets of 3 dc and 4 ch-2 spaces).

  3. Round 3:

    Sl st to the first ch-2 space. Ch 3 (counts as the first dc), 2 dc into the same ch-2 space, ch 2, 3 dc into the same ch-2 space (first corner made). *3 dc into the space between the sets of dc from the previous round, 3 dc into the next ch-2 space, ch 2, 3 dc into the same ch-2 space*, repeat from * to * 2 more times, 3 dc into the space between the sets of dc. Join with a sl st to the top of the beginning ch 3. (You should have 12 sets of 3 dc and 4 ch-2 spaces).

  4. Round 4:

    Sl st to the first ch-2 space. Ch 3 (counts as the first dc), 2 dc into the same ch-2 space, ch 2, 3 dc into the same ch-2 space (first corner made). *3 dc into the space between the sets of dc from the previous round, 3 dc into the next space between the sets of dc, 3 dc into the next ch-2 space, ch 2, 3 dc into the same ch-2 space*, repeat from * to * 2 more times, 3 dc into each of the next 2 spaces between the sets of dc. Join with a sl st to the top of the beginning ch 3. (You should have 16 sets of 3 dc and 4 ch-2 spaces).

  5. Round 5:

    Sl st to the first ch-2 space. Ch 3 (counts as the first dc), 2 dc into the same ch-2 space, ch 2, 3 dc into the same ch-2 space (first corner made). *3 dc into each of the next 3 spaces between the sets of dc from the previous round, 3 dc into the next ch-2 space, ch 2, 3 dc into the same ch-2 space*, repeat from * to * 2 more times, 3 dc into each of the next 3 spaces between the sets of dc. Join with a sl st to the top of the beginning ch 3. (You should have 20 sets of 3 dc and 4 ch-2 spaces).

  6. Round 6:

    Sl st to the first ch-2 space. Ch 3 (counts as the first dc), 2 dc into the same ch-2 space, ch 2, 3 dc into the same ch-2 space (first corner made). *3 dc into each of the next 4 spaces between the sets of dc from the previous round, 3 dc into the next ch-2 space, ch 2, 3 dc into the same ch-2 space*, repeat from * to * 2 more times, 3 dc into each of the next 4 spaces between the sets of dc. Join with a sl st to the top of the beginning ch 3. Fasten off and weave in the ends.
